About Us

We are the best dance school

Studio84 school of dance is one of the leading dance schools in Pune, MH. The dance school was established in 2006. boasting a team of skilled, enthusiastic, experienced, and dedicated teachers who provide personalised attention to each student. They offer diverse styles and genres, and Studio84 school of dance is a hub for dance and fitness enthusiasts. Their studio presents comprehensive dance and fitness programs, including Jive, Salsa, Hip Hop, Bollywood, Garba, Belly Dance, Zumba, and more. With a commitment to reliable services, Studio84 school of dance ensures the best rates and guidance tailored to individual needs. Their variety of dance programs caters to everyone, and classes are available from Monday to Saturday, accommodating diverse schedules. Their offerings go beyond traditional dance classes, incorporating fitness programs into their curriculum. They provide courses for all ages and abilities, from beginners to intermediate and advanced levels. Studio84 school of dance is dedicated to imparting the joy of dance to enthusiasts with a passion for movement and expression.
Be part of the Studio84 school of dance family! Studio84 school of dance , a dance academy, offers various courses for dance enthusiasts. We specialize in Garba and Folk dance and the journey of learning this dance form is fun, easy, energetic, and entertaining!
